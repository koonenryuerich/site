About Us

Community Service Council We are currently in the process of designing a Community Service Council, a council comprised of student leaders who will serve to oversee Community Service activities in the Upper School. The council will help select service opportunities, provide project leadership and encourage student participation in community service. Community Service Council meetings wil be open for all Upper School students to attend and to participate in, whether or not they hold positions on the council. After the Community Service Council has been officially created, elections will be held to select student representatives from each grade level. Stay tuned for more information!
